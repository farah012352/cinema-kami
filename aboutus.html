About Us

Welcome to RedVibe Cinema, where movie magic comes to life! At RedVibe Cinema, we are committed to providing an unforgettable cinematic experience for our guests. With state-of-the-art technology, comfortable seating, and a wide selection of movies, we strive to be your go-to destination for entertainment.

Our mission is to create a space where people can come together to enjoy stories that inspire, entertain, and connect. From the latest blockbusters to timeless classics, we have something for everyone.

We also offer a variety of delicious snacks and beverages to enhance your movie experience. Thank you for choosing RedVibe Cinema. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the show!

Our History

At RedVibe Cinema, it is not just about movies but a total entertainment experience! Since 2025, we have welcomed millions of guests through our doors, offering the widest range of cinematic experiences in Malaysia.

Innovation is at the core of everything we do. We're redefining the moviegoing experience with our INDULGE halls, offering a luxurious and ultra-comfortable cinema experience. For a more relaxed outing, our Beanie halls provide cozy beanbag pods for the ultimate comfort. And for families, our Junior halls create a fun and engaging environment where kids can play, laugh, and learn. At RedVibe Cinema, the possibilities are endless – we’re always pushing the limits to make your movie experience better than ever.

RedVibe Cinema operates 328 screens in 40 locations throughout Malaysia. RedVibe Cinema can be found in major shopping centers across Kuala Lumpur, Selangor, Negeri Sembilan, Perak, Penang, Johor, Terengganu, and Sarawak.

Where Are We Located?

We are located at 40 locations with 325 screens and more than 53,000 seats all over Malaysia, including major shopping centres in Kuala Lumpur, Selangor, Negeri Sembilan, Perak, Penang, Johor, Terengganu, and Sarawak.
